Academic Suport at UMSI

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering a wide array of resources and services. Our academic advising team provides personalized guidance to help you navigate your curriculum, select courses, and address any academic challenges or questions you may encounter during your studies. UMSI students also have access to the extensive collections and study spaces within the University Library system, offering both digital and physical resources essential for research and coursework. Additionally, our specialized tutoring services and the UMSI Writing Center are available to support you in mastering complex subjects and enhancing your written communication skills. By utilizing these academic resources, UMSI students can thrive in their studies and achieve their educational goals.

MSI Degree Requirements

To earn a Master of Science in Information at the School of Information, you must complete:

  1. A minimum of 48 credit hours of graduate coursework
  2. A minimum grade average of C- (3.0 minimum cumulative GPA required), including required coursework

Note: within the 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, a minimum of 39 credits must be coursework taken within UMSI (SI credit).

Registering for Classes

Step 1: Planning your schedule

To effectively plan your upcoming term, start by visiting Atlas to review historical course information, then check your Degree Audit to track your course progress and remaining requirements. Next, consult the UMSI Course Catalog for the latest semester offerings, descriptions, and syllabi. Use Schedule Builder to create a sample schedule, and finally, meet with an Academic Advisor to refine and confirm your plan.

Step 2: Backpacking

To register for your courses, first sign into Wolverine Access and navigate to "Student Business" under the "Students" tab. From there, click on "Student Center." Add your selected courses for the term to your Backpack, as this is required for registration. Itâ€™s advisable to add extra courses to your Backpack as backup options in case your preferred courses fill up quickly.

Step 3: Registration

To complete your course registration, sign into Wolverine Access and navigate to "Student Business" under the "Students" tab, then click on "Student Center." From there, select the checkboxes next to the courses you've added to your Backpack and click "Proceed to Step 2 of 3." After that, click "Finish Registration" and confirm that there is a green checkmark next to each course for which you intend to register. If you see a red "X," review the notification and proceed accordingly. Finally, you can view your schedule by selecting "My Course Schedule" within the Student Center.

Academic Success and Tutoring

The UMSI Academic Success Team, as part of the Academic Programs & Student Life Team, works to holistically support students, both academically and personally. The Academic Success team oversees UMSI tutoring and academic skill-building programs, in addition to supporting students in navigating their time at UMSI.
